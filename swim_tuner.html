How this works

For each date you supply, this tool fetches the Open-Meteo archive (wind, marine/wave, SST) and runs your unchanged production calcScore() — no scoring params are swept. It then pulls the Bold & Beautiful blog post for that date (via your Cloudflare Worker /blog route) so you can confirm a per-metric swim-sentiment label. The scatter at the bottom plots each metric's raw value against your labels, so you can read off where the zone break-points should sit. Days the current thresholds misclassify are flagged in red.
